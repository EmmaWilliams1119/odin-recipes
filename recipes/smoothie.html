Smoothie

Description

This recipe is for a delicious home made smoothie, which is fruity, smooth and healthy.

This is a perfect recipe for hot summers day.

Ingredients

  • Frozen mixed berries
  • Banana
  • Greek Yoghurt
  • Almond Milk
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to the blender one by one.
  2. Add any other ingredients wanted but not listed
  3. Blend for 1 minute until smooth.
  4. Continue to blend to reach your preferred consistency.
  5. Pour into glass, serve and enjoy!
